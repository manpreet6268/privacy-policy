Welcome to the EMI Calculator app developed by Altqube. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Our EMI Calculator app is a simple financial tool that performs calculations entirely on your device. We do not collect any of the following:

The EMI Calculator works fully offline on your device. When you enter loan amount, interest rate, and duration, all calculations are done locally on your phone. No data is sent to any server or third party.

7. Data Storage

We do not store any user data on our servers because we do not collect any data in the first place. Any values you enter in the app exist only in your phone's memory while the app is open and are not saved anywhere after you close the app.
